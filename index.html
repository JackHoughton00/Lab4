Welcome to Ottawa Physiotherapy Clinic

About Us

Located at 50 Rideau Street, we are a state of the start physiotherapy clinic located in the heart of the Ottawa. Founded in 2013, we are commited to giving each of our patients the highest quality care possible.
We specialize in working with athletes of all levels to assist them perform to their highest posssible level.
We also offer a variety of different services and our team will work with you to create the best course of action to best help you!


Our Services

We offer cutsom knee, elbow, ankle, shoulder and wrist braces. We will work with you to create a personalized and unique support brace to help with injury rehabilitation. For pricing, you will need to schedule a free consultation with one of our physiotherapists to get an estimate.

Our expert physiotherapists will tailor a rehabilitation plan specific to you and will work with you throughout your rehabilition process to ensure you come back from your injury stronger! Our rates start at $50 a session, schedule a free consultation with one of our physiotherapists to create a rehabilition plan to better estimate the total cost of your therapy.

We offer cryotherapy to give you the best recovery possible. Our cryotherapy sessions are $40 each, there is no need to schedule a consultation appointment prior to your first session and your first session is free!

Come see one of our acupuncture specialists to help treat issues such as migranes, arthritis, chronic pain and stress! Starting at $75 a session, first schedule a consultation appointment with one of our specialists to better determine the best course of action. Your first three sessions are free of charge!

We offer many different types of massages including, deep tissue massages, sports massages and trigger point massages. Come see one of our masseuses today to determine the best course of action for you! For first time patients, your first massage is free of charge. However, subesquent appoinments will be $100 a session.
